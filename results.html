The Situation

A Houston roofing contractor with a solid reputation and years of experience was buried in local search. Despite doing quality work, they were invisible in the local pack for their most important searches and almost entirely dependent on referrals for new business.

What We Did

Full Google Business Profile audit and rebuild. Category correction to Roofing Contractor from the generic Contractor. Service area configuration across the full Houston metro. The Situation
