A Straight Answer

Why Most Local SEO
Campaigns Fail

Most agencies won't tell you this. We will — because understanding why SEO fails is the first step to making it work.

✗
They optimize for keywords instead of entities

Modern search engines don't just match keywords — they resolve entities. If Google can't clearly identify who you are, what you do, and where you do it, no amount of keyword stuffing will save you. Most agencies are still fighting a 2015 battle with 2015 tactics.

✗
They ignore the infrastructure and chase tactics

Blog posts without internal linking. Citations without consistency. A Google Business Profile left half-empty. SEO without a proper technical and structural foundation is like building on sand. Short-term ranking spikes don't compound — authority does.

✗
They're invisible to AI search — and don't even know it

AI Overviews, ChatGPT, and Perplexity are now answering questions your customers used to Google. If your content isn't structured for generative engines — no schema, no entity signals, no authoritative sourcing — you simply don't exist in AI search. That's not a future problem. It's happening right now.

What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

GEO structures your content so AI-powered search engines like Google's AI Overview, Perplexity, and ChatGPT cite your business in generated answers. It builds on traditional SEO with structured data, entity optimization, and authoritative content signals that AI systems use to select trustworthy sources.

What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?

AEO optimizes your content to appear in direct answer formats — Google's featured snippets, People Also Ask boxes, and voice search results. It focuses on clear, structured answers to questions your customers are asking, placing you at position zero above organic results.

How does SEO connect to AI search in 2026?

SEO is the foundational layer that makes AI search work. When Google's AI Overview or ChatGPT generate an answer, they pull from content that has been indexed and ranked by search engines. Without strong SEO fundamentals, your business cannot appear in AI-generated answers — no matter how much AI-specific optimization you layer on top.

Do you work with small local businesses?

Absolutely. NSYGHT specializes in local service businesses of all sizes — from solo operators in roofing, dumpster rental, and party rental to multi-location franchises and law firms. Our strategies are built for businesses where ranking in local searches directly drives calls and revenue.

How long does SEO take to show results?

Honest answer: meaningful ranking movement takes 3–6 months, and sustained authority builds over 6–12 months. Paid ads drive leads immediately while SEO compounds in the background. We'll tell you exactly what to expect for your market and competition level on our first call — no vague promises.
